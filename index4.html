What is Audio Alchemist?

Audio Alchemist is a new dynamic online platform that allows you to further enjoy your favorite songs. Search for the lyrics on our lyric search section or enjoy the experience on the music visualizer, where you can upload and enjoy the visual experience while you listen.

How does it work?

When you search for music or upload a music file, our application uses audio analysis algorithms to extract audio features such as beats, melody, and harmony. We then map these audio features to visual elements such as colors, shapes, and patterns, creating a unique visual representation of the music.
